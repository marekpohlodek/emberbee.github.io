03 / STUDIO

One person, one bench, one bee.

Solo · since 2025

I build the games I wished existed when I was eleven — strange little worlds with generous rules and characters who remember.

Ember Bee is a one-person studio operating out of a small apartment with one keyboard and one extremely opinionated cat. Design, code, art, and audio under one roof — sometimes that means slower, but it always means whole.
